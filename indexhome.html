Introducing MyBud

Music is an art form that has always brought people together. With MyBud, we're taking that to a whole new level. Our app allows you to connect with your friends through your love of music in ways you've never imagined before. MyBud is more than just a music scrobbling app, It's a social experience that lets you share your favorite tracks, discover new artists, and explore different genres with your friends. And with our real-time listening stats, you can see what your friends are listening to and how you stack up against them. It's like having your own personal DJ right in your pocket!

Show off your style

MyBud offers a wide selection of unique and eye-catching avatars that allow users to express their individuality and personal style. From colorful cartoon characters to sleek and modern designs, there is an avatar for everyone on MyBud. Users can easily customize their profile by choosing their favorite avatar and updating their display name. This feature not only adds a fun and personal touch to the app but also allows users to show off their personality and connect with others who share similar interests.
